WeGesture

Gestures were just catching on when I made this prototype. I felt it was unfair for designer and developers at Apple or any company to decide how people would gesture for an action on a device. I hope that these companies did something similar to this prototype.

This prototype asks users to gesture for a given set of common actions: forward, backward, copy, paste, play, pause, etc. After users gesture for the action, an image is saved of their gesture. That's it, that's all I needed from the users, just their idea.

Once I had enough images saved on my server, I made a microsite to showcase everyone's gesture choices! The findings were pretty crazy.
